❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I clear the GeM Vendor Assessment without rejection?

Vendor Assessment by RITES/QCI requires precise documentation including ITR verification, ISO certificates, manufacturing process flowcharts, and machinery details. We conduct an internal pre-audit to fix all discrepancies before final submission.

What is the difference between Reseller and OEM accounts?

An OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) can list unique products, issue Vendor Authorization Codes (VAC), and control brand listings. Resellers can only pair products under approved OEM brands.
