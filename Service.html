Frequently Asked Questions - GeM Portal Services

GeM portal par primary registration kaise karein? +

To successfully complete registration, you require an active Aadhaar number linked with a mobile number, PAN card details, active bank account documents, and an active MSME/Udyam certificate to establish an error-free vendor seller profile account.

GeM portal par verified OEM kaishe banein? +

To become a registered OEM, you must register your custom brand name or apply under an unregistered category. Once the trademark parameters are validated, you must successfully clear the QCI Desktop Assessment and Factory Video Assessment audits.

Portal par fast brand approval kaishe lein? +

Brand approval can be secured by submitting the dynamic Upload Brand Registry application panel along with a clear Trademark registration certificate or a valid Brand Authorization Letter from the original manufacturer.
