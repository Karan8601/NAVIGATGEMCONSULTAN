The Journey: From Compliance Friction to Enterprise Engine

My professional journey began with a commitment to mastering the complexities of government procurement. Between 2021 and 2024, during my tenure as a GeM Operations & Office Coordinator at New Regent Trading Company, I developed a deep understanding of bid lifecycles, workflow optimization, and the critical importance of vendor compliance. Managing technical and financial bids with a 75% qualification success rate, I realized that many manufacturers struggle not due to product quality, but due to the procedural friction of the GeM portal.

This realization led to the inception of NavigateGeM, a consulting firm based in Lucknow, dedicated to demystifying the GeM ecosystem for businesses across India. As the CEO and Founder, I have since transitioned into an expert GeM Portal & OEM Consultant, helping brands execute end-to-end OEM registration and successfully navigate the rigorous RITES and GeM Team vendor assessment processes.

The Growth Mandate

Today, NavigateGeM is not just a consultancy; it is an engine for growth that serves clients nationwide. My focus remains on delivering strategic solutions—whether it is managing bulk cataloguing, ensuring ISO/BIS/CE compliance, or providing real-time support during reverse auctions.
