OEM Categories & Specific Mandatory Documents

1. Standard OEM

Proprietary manufacturers who own the machinery lines and manufacture products inside their own registered factory blocks.

Mandatory Documents:
  • Valid Factory License / DIC Registry
  • Plant & Machinery Asset Logs
  • Electricity Bill of Industrial Unit
  • Process Flow Chart of Production

2. Deemed OEM

Entities that don't directly own physical manufacturing units but hold sovereign statutory public utility rights or core technology patents.

Mandatory Documents:
  • Govt Establishment / PSU Allotment Order
  • Patent Registration Allotment Proof
  • Technology Transfer (ToT) Legal Copy
  • Statutory Exemption Gazetted Order

3. Reseller OEM

Traders or distributors authorized to manage inventory dashboards under dynamic authority code links mapped from the master OEM portal.

Mandatory Documents:
  • Manufacturer Authorization Form (MAF)
  • Active OEM Linkage Dashboard Approval
  • Tender Specific Authority Letter
  • Valid Authorization Expiry Ledger

4. Contract Manufacturing

Brands that own the product trademark registration but outsource the complete physical production assembly lines to a third-party plant unit.

Mandatory Documents:
  • Registered Trademark Certificate
  • Legal Contract Manufacturing Agreement
  • Factory License of the Production Plant
  • Quality SLA Signatures Matrix copy
