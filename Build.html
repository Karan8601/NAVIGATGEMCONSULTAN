GeM OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) Structural Types

Standard OEM

Proprietary manufacturers who own the machinery and manufacture products inside their own registered assembly nodes.

  • Requires Trademark Registry
  • Requires Factory Operational License
  • Requires Detailed Machinery logs

Deemed OEM

Entities that don't directly own the machinery nodes but hold massive statutory public utility rights or exclusive authority permissions.

  • Government undertakings/PSUs
  • Proprietary tech patent owners
  • Exempted institutional setups

Reseller OEM

TrDistribution inventory elements under direct dynamic authority codes granted by the main OEM dashboard.

  • Requires MAF Letter
  • Requires active OEM linkage hooks
  • No factory audits needed

Contract Manufacturing

Brands that own the product trademark but lease the physical production lines from a third-party factory unit under a legal contract matrix.

  • Requires Registered Trademark
  • Requires Legal Contract Agreement
  • Audits factory execution plant site
